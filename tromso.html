About Tromsø

Tromsø sits at 69 degrees north — well above the Arctic Circle — in the Troms og Finnmark county of northern Norway. With a population of around 77,000, it is the largest city in Arctic Norway and serves as the primary gateway to the High Arctic. The surrounding landscape is dramatically mountainous, with the Lyngen Alps rising to over 1,800 metres to the east and the Tromsøysund strait separating the island city from the mainland.

The Tromsø region is statistically one of the best locations on Earth for northern lights viewing, owing to its position directly beneath the auroral oval — the ring-shaped zone around the geomagnetic poles where aurora activity is most frequent. Between late October and mid-March, the KP index (a measure of geomagnetic activity) regularly reaches levels sufficient for visible aurora, and cloud cover permitting, sightings are possible on the majority of clear nights.

Faraway & Fine operates three private glass-roofed wilderness cabins on a south-facing hillside 14 kilometres from Tromsø city centre, at an elevation of 420 metres. The cabins are positioned above the cloud inversion layer that sits over the fjord on most winter nights, providing clear sky conditions when the city below is cloud-covered. Each cabin sleeps two guests and is attended by a dedicated local guide throughout the stay.

Getting There

Tromsø Airport (TOS) is served by direct flights from London Heathrow (Norwegian, SAS), London Gatwick (Norwegian), and Oslo (SAS, Norwegian). Flight time from London is approximately 3 hours. Faraway & Fine arranges all transfers from the airport to the wilderness cabins — approximately 35 minutes by private car. The transfer includes a pre-arrival briefing on aurora viewing and cold-weather clothing.

Guests arriving late may wish to stay one night at the Clarion Hotel The Edge in Tromsø city centre, which has panoramic harbour views and is adjacent to the Tromsø Science Centre's planetarium — Faraway & Fine can arrange this on request at an additional cost of approximately £220 per night.
