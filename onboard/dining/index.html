Six Dining Concepts

Onboard
Dining

From Michelin-influenced tasting menus in the main saloon to open-fire cooking on Arctic beaches — dining is central to every Faraway & Fine voyage.

ℹ Dining concepts vary by vessel. Not all experiences below are available on every ship — consult your Travel Director when booking.

The Saloon Table

MV Nordlys · MV Kvitbjørn

The main dining experience aboard both motor vessels. A Nordic tasting menu of 5–7 courses prepared by the onboard chef using ingredients sourced each morning from farms and fishing boats. Served at the communal Norwegian oak table in the main saloon. Wine pairing available.

Deck Barbecue

All vessels · Summer season

On warm evenings at anchor in protected fjord bays, the chef moves the cooking outside. Whole fish over charcoal, flatbreads, and seasonal vegetables. Served on the sundeck with Norwegian craft beer. Weather dependent — offered on approximately 60% of summer nights.

Chef's Table

MV Nordlys only

By request, the chef opens the galley for a private chef's table experience for up to two guests. A seven-course menu with the chef explaining each dish and its provenance. Available on voyages of 5 nights or more. Pre-booking required; £120 per person supplement.

Cockpit Breakfast

SY Isfjord

Breakfast served in the cockpit of the SY Isfjord while underway. Fresh bread, Norwegian cheeses, cured salmon, and egg dishes. The standard morning experience aboard the Isfjord — weather permitting, always served outside.

Arctic Beach Fire

MV Kvitbjørn · Winter expeditions

On select evenings during Arctic expeditions, the guide leads guests ashore to a protected beach where an open fire is prepared. Reindeer stew, flatbreads, and cloudberry dessert. Not available on all itineraries — confirmed by the guide based on conditions.

Shore Dining Programme

All vessels · All seasons

On one evening per voyage, Faraway & Fine arranges a private dinner at a shore-side restaurant — a family-run fjordside inn, a Michelin-recognised Gothenburg address, or a candlelit Copenhagen townhouse kitchen. Included in all programmes at no additional cost.
