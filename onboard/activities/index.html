Expert-Led

Activities &
Expeditions

Every activity on a Faraway & Fine voyage is led by a specialist — a certified sea kayak guide, an ornithologist, a Sami musher, a nature photographer.

Sea Kayaking

All vessels · Summer

Daily kayaking from the vessel in protected fjord or archipelago waters. Equipment and waterproof clothing provided. Guided by a certified BCU-qualified sea kayak instructor.

Fjord Hiking

MV Nordlys · Norway

Guided ascents to fjord viewpoints including Seven Sisters (Geirangerfjord), Reinebringen (Lofoten), and Nærøyfjord viewpoints. Moderate fitness required. Equipment provided.

Dog-Sledding

MV Kvitbjørn · Winter

Full-day expedition across the Lyngen Alps with a Sami musher and a team of 8 Alaskan Huskies. Approximately 30km. All cold-weather equipment provided. Minimum age 14.

Wildlife Safari

MV Kvitbjørn · Year-round

RIB excursions led by a marine biologist or ornithologist. Sea eagle observation in Lofoten, whale watching off Andøya, and seabird colony visits on Røst.

Snowshoeing

MV Kvitbjørn · Winter

Half-day snowshoe expeditions from the Tromsø aurora cabins to a frozen waterfall and an elevated viewpoint at 680m. Night snowshoeing under headtorch on clear nights.

Northern Lights Photography

MV Kvitbjørn · Winter

Private photography sessions with a professional nature photographer on clear nights. Camera settings instruction included. Supplement: £350 per stay.
