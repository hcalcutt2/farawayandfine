Wellness at Sea

Spa &
Wellness

In-cabin treatments, Arctic cold immersion, and a barrel sauna programme aboard MV Kvitbjørn.

Deep Tissue Massage

£95 · 75 minutes · All vessels

Full-body deep tissue massage performed in your cabin by a qualified therapist. Available on request on all voyages of 3 nights or more. Book 24 hours in advance via your Travel Director.

Hot Stone Massage

£85 · 60 minutes · All vessels

Swedish-influenced hot stone massage using smooth Norwegian river stones heated to 58°C. Combines relaxation massage with targeted deep tissue work on the back and shoulders.

Nordic Facial

£70 · 45 minutes · All vessels

Deep-cleansing facial using Norwegian skincare products formulated for cold-climate skin. Includes a 10-minute scalp and neck massage.

Arctic Sauna Programme

Complimentary · MV Kvitbjørn only

The MV Kvitbjørn carries a portable barrel sauna deployed on shore at anchor. The programme includes 3 rounds of 15 minutes in the sauna followed by cold fjord immersion. Weather dependent.
