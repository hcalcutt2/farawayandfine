About
Faraway & Fine™

A fictional luxury travel company founded by two expedition guides who believed there was a better way to experience the Nordic north.

How It Started

Faraway & Fine™ was founded in 2019 by Alistair Brennan and Ingrid Halvorsen — a Scottish expedition guide and a Norwegian marine biologist who met on a research vessel in the Barents Sea in 2016. After several years leading commercial expedition groups across the Norwegian fjords, Svalbard, and the Swedish archipelago, they concluded that the existing luxury travel market was doing Scandinavia a disservice: selling it as a backdrop for convenience rather than a place to be understood on its own terms.

The company launched with a single vessel — a converted fishing boat called the Hildur, since retired — and three itineraries. In its first year of operation it carried 34 guests. By 2022 it had expanded to the current fleet of three vessels and a portfolio of land-based experiences across Norway, Sweden, and Denmark. The Hildur was sold in 2021 and replaced by the MV Kvitbjørn.

Our History

2019

Faraway & Fine founded in Edinburgh and Tromsø. First vessel, MV Hildur, acquired. Three itineraries launched. 34 guests in year one.

2020

Operations suspended March–September due to Covid-19. Used the period to develop the Copenhagen and Stockholm land programmes. MV Nordlys acquired and restoration begun at Ålesund.

2021

Operations resumed May. MV Hildur sold. MV Nordlys restoration completed and entered service August. Aurora cabin programme launched above Tromsø (initially two cabins; expanded to three in 2023).

2022

SY Isfjord acquired from a Swedish sailing family and refitted at Gothenburg. Sweden programmes launched. Total guests carried since founding passes 400.

2023

MV Kvitbjørn acquired from the University of Tromsø research fleet. Third aurora cabin added. Denmark programmes expanded. First Svalbard charter programme trialled.

2024

Faraway & Fine carries its 800th guest. Lofoten winter programme formalised as a standalone product. This website launched.

Where We Are Based

Faraway & Fine's administrative office is in Edinburgh, Scotland. Our operational bases are in Ålesund (MV Nordlys), Gothenburg (SY Isfjord), and Tromsø (MV Kvitbjørn and the aurora cabins). All guest communication is handled from Edinburgh; all in-destination logistics are managed from the relevant operational base.
