Indian Ocean · Wilderness

Maldives Private Atoll

7 Nights · Year-round Departure

Faraway & Fine has secured exclusive access to a private 12-villa island at Faridhoo Atoll in the North Malé region — unavailable to the general public through any other channel. Your villa sits 40 metres over a protected marine lagoon with direct ocean access.

The island hosts a resident marine biologist who leads private snorkelling and dive excursions to a nearby manta ray cleaning station, accessible only via a private speedboat. Evenings include a personal chef who adjusts menus daily to your preferences, sourcing ingredients from sustainable Maldivian farms.

  • Return floatplane transfers from Malé Airport (Velana International)
  • Full-board dining with a private chef; menus adapted daily
  • Two-hour manta ray snorkelling excursion with marine biologist
  • Sunset dhoni cruise with champagne and canapés
  • In-villa spa treatments (2 per guest)
  • 24-hour Faraway & Fine Travel Director support
£12,400 per person · based on two guests

Scandinavia · Yacht Charter

Norwegian Fjords Private Yacht

7 Days · February, June & September

Charter the MV Hjørundfjord — a 42-metre expedition yacht purpose-built for the Norwegian coast — and navigate the most dramatic waterways in Europe. The vessel carries up to six guests with a crew of eight including a private chef, expedition guide, and cinematographer.

The route passes through Geirangerfjord (a UNESCO World Heritage Site), Nærøyfjord (the narrowest in Europe), and the remote Hjørundfjord. In February, the route is optimised for northern lights viewing; in June, the midnight sun. A kayaking guide is aboard for all departures.

  • Exclusive use of MV Hjørundfjord (up to 6 guests)
  • Full crew: captain, chef, expedition guide, cinematographer
  • All meals prepared with locally sourced Norwegian produce
  • Daily kayaking, hiking, and fjord swimming opportunities
  • Northern lights wake-up call service (February departures)
  • Complimentary drone footage of your voyage
£18,600 per couple · whole-yacht charter

South America · Expedition

Patagonia Southern Ice Expedition

10 Days · June, July & August

A private expedition into some of the most remote terrain on Earth. Beginning in Puerto Natales, your Faraway & Fine expedition team guides you through Torres del Paine National Park, across the Southern Patagonian Ice Field, and into the cave systems of the Río Serrano canyon.

Accommodation is at Faraway & Fine's own network of eco glamping sites — four-poster beds inside weatherproof canvas tents with wood-burning stoves and private outdoor baths overlooking the ice. Helicopter transfers are used for ice field crossings.

  • Private expedition team: lead guide, safety specialist, photographer
  • Helicopter glacier transfers over the Southern Ice Field
  • Private eco-glamping at three Faraway & Fine sites (no shared facilities)
  • Guided kayaking on Lago Grey among floating icebergs
  • Full board, including expedition-style hot meals in remote locations
  • Sat-phone and emergency evacuation protocol
£9,200 per person · minimum 2 guests

East Asia · Cultural Immersion

Kyoto Private Heritage Retreat

5 Nights · March–April (Sakura) or November (Koyo)

Faraway & Fine's Kyoto programme centres on a private machiya — a restored 1890s townhouse in the Nishijin weaver's district, sleeping four. The property is unavailable to the public and has been prepared exclusively for Faraway & Fine guests by the family who has owned it for four generations.

Your Faraway & Fine Cultural Director, a former professor of Japanese history at Kyoto University, arranges private access to Maruyama Park before dawn, a personal audience with the master of a third-generation sake brewery, and a private performance by a maiko (apprentice geisha) in a Gion ochaya teahouse.

  • Five nights in a private machiya townhouse, Nishijin district
  • Pre-dawn private access to Maruyama Park and Philosopher's Path
  • Private audience and tasting at a third-generation sake brewery
  • Maiko performance in a private Gion teahouse
  • Hands-on wagashi (Japanese sweet) making with a confectionery master
  • Dedicated Cultural Director throughout the stay
£6,800 per person · based on two guests
