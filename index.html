Our Philosophy

The Nordic Way,
Done Properly

Faraway & Fine™ was built around a single conviction: that Scandinavia rewards those who go slowly, go privately, and go with someone who truly knows the way. Not a tour group. Not a package. A journey shaped entirely around you.

Each journey is led by a dedicated Nordic Travel Director — a local expert who holds the keys to places that simply don't appear on any map or booking platform.

  • Private fjord crossings by traditional wooden faering boat
  • After-hours access to Scandinavian design museums and galleries
  • Stays in historic Swedish manor houses and Norwegian stave lodges

Four Ways to Journey

🛥️

Fjord Yacht Charters

Private expedition yachts navigating Geirangerfjord, Nærøyfjord, and the Lofoten archipelago. Crews of six with onboard chefs serving fresh Nordic seafood.

Northern lights camps above Tromsø, dog-sledding across the Finnmark plateau, and private ice-fishing lodges on frozen Norwegian lakes.
